FreeSchedule

Info

A site for building the optimal work schedule for the next week for a group of employees based on the constraints they expressed and imposed by the manager.

It helps managers spend less time on planning and focus on other tasks, and also makes schedules more optimal, which makes the schedule more acceptable to employees.

FreeSchedule's mission is to free managers from routine planning and provide teams with transparent, fair and flexible work schedules.
